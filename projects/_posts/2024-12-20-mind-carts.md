### Details
- 120 levels (104 Normal + 16 Hard)
- Written in C++ (compiled to WebAssembly)
- Built with a custom engine and using the following dependencies:
	- Web: FreeType and Emscripten
	- Desktop: FreeType, GLFW and Glad
